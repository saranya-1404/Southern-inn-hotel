About Southern Inn

Our Story

Southern Inn is a boutique luxury hotel inspired by the elegance of French architecture and the peaceful charm of Puducherry. Every space has been thoughtfully designed using soft pastel interiors, creating a warm and relaxing atmosphere for every guest.

Whether you choose a Jacuzzi Room, a Sea View Room or a spacious Family Room, our goal is to provide exceptional comfort, personalized hospitality and unforgettable experiences.
